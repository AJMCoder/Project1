Why play Tennis?

Feel healthy

Tennis is an aerobic sport, which means it is one of the best forms of exercise due to its stop-start movement style. Studies have shown that regular continuous exercise helps improve your overall cardiovascular health.

Feel stronger

Playing tennis makes you stronger, faster and more flexible. The intense bursts of activity is great for your heart and also great for you muscles, as you are constantly using different muscle groups like your back, forearms, core muscles and legs.

Get involved

Playing tennis is sociable – it can be a fun activity to do with a friend or family member and it’s easy to meet like-minded people at your local club.

Feel challenged

Whether you knock a ball around with a friend once a week or compete regularly in a league, committing to training and seeing your skills improve is very rewarding. Many people find that this reinforces the value of hard work and helps then visualise their progress.
